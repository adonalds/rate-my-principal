Welcome to Rate My Principal

This version is for teachers and school staff to share anonymous feedback about principals.

Sort reviews by:

Reminder: Please do not include any personal details that could identify you or others. Keep comments general and professional. Reviews are public and anonymous only if you keep them anonymous.

How to write a helpful review

  • Focus on patterns over time, not one bad (or great) day.
  • Talk about communication, support, fairness, and school climate.
  • Avoid personal attacks, threats, or naming teachers or students.
  • Share what it feels like to work under this principal, not private details.
