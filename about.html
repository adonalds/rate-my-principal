About Rate My Principal

Rate My Principal was created to give teachers and school staff a simple, honest way to share feedback about school leadership and school culture.

The goal is to provide transparency, encourage improvement, and help educators better understand what it is like to work at a particular school under a particular principal.

Reviews are anonymous as long as you avoid including personal identifying details about yourself or anyone else. Please keep comments professional, respectful, and focused on school leadership or school environment — never individuals other than the principal.

Safety & Privacy Reminder

To protect everyone’s privacy and safety, please DO NOT include:

  • Student names
  • Your own name or any identifying details
  • Teacher names (this site is for reviewing principals only)
  • Specific incidents that clearly reveal who you are
  • Phone numbers, email addresses, or home addresses

Reviews that clearly violate these guidelines may be removed in the future if moderation tools are added.
