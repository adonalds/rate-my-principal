About Rate My Principal

Rate My Principal is a simple, anonymous place for teachers and school staff to share honest feedback about school leadership and school climate.

The goal is to help educators understand what it is like to work under a particular principal and within a particular school environment.

Who This Site Is For

This site is intended primarily for:

  • Teachers
  • Support staff and other school employees

A separate space for parents may be added in the future, but this version is focused on the experience of staff working in the building every day.

What This Site Is (and Is Not)

This site is:

  • A place to share honest opinions about leadership and school climate
  • A way to gather staff perspectives in one place
  • A tool to help educators make more informed choices

This site is not:

  • An official rating from any school district or government agency
  • A place to make threats, harass people, or spread hate
  • A place to post private information about staff, students, or families
  • A place to report emergencies or immediate safety concerns

Anonymity and Privacy

Important: Reviews are anonymous only if you keep them anonymous. The site does not ask for your name or email, but what you write could still identify you.

To protect your privacy, please avoid:

  • Describing yourself in a way that clearly points to one person (for example, “the only 7th grade science teacher in the building”).
  • Listing exact years and roles if they make you obviously identifiable.
  • Including phone numbers, email addresses, or other contact information.
  • Naming other teachers, staff members, students, or families in your comments.

What to Write

Helpful reviews focus on patterns and behaviors, not personal attacks. For example:

  • “The principal communicates expectations clearly and is visible in the building.”
  • “Staff input is sometimes requested, but decisions often feel top-down.”
  • “The school climate is generally positive, but staff morale has declined over the last few years.”

What Not to Write

Please do not post:

  • Threats, harassment, or abusive language
  • Slurs or hate speech
  • Accusations of illegal activity
  • Private information about specific staff, students, or families
  • Full names of teachers or students who are not the principal being reviewed

Reviews that clearly violate these guidelines may be removed if moderation tools are added in the future.

Can Someone Figure Out Who I Am?

No system can guarantee that a review can never be connected to a specific person. Small schools, unique roles, or very specific details can make it easier for someone to guess who wrote a review.

If you are worried about being identified, keep your review focused on experiences and patterns, not on details that only one person would know.

Content Responsibility

All content on this site is user-submitted opinion. The site does not verify or endorse individual reviews. Each reviewer is responsible for the statements they choose to share.

If a formal moderation or “report review” process is added in the future, instructions on how to request a review or removal of content will be posted here.
