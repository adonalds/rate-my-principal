About Rate My Principal

Rate My Principal is a simple, anonymous place for teachers and school staff to share honest feedback about school leadership and school climate.

Who This Site Is For

This site is designed primarily for:

  • Teachers
  • Support staff and other school employees

The goal is to help educators understand what it is like to work under a particular principal and within a particular school environment.

What You Can Review

You can share feedback about:

  • The principal’s leadership, communication, and decision-making
  • The overall school climate and culture
  • How supported staff feel in their roles
Important: Do not include any details that could identify you or another individual. Keep comments general and professional.

Anonymity and Privacy

Reviews on this site are meant to be anonymous. The site does not ask for your name, email address, or any login information. However, your anonymity also depends on what you choose to write.

Please avoid:

  • Stating your exact role if it makes you easily identifiable (for example, “the only 7th grade science teacher”)
  • Mentioning specific classrooms, years, or other details that clearly point to you
  • Including phone numbers, email addresses, or other personal contact information

What to Write (and What Not to Write)

Helpful examples:

  • “The principal communicates expectations clearly and is visible in the building.”
  • “Discipline policies are inconsistent and staff are often left out of the loop.”
  • “The school climate is generally positive, but staff morale has declined over the last few years.”

Please do not include:

  • Threats or abusive language
  • Accusations of illegal activity
  • Personal attacks on other staff members, students, or families
  • Names of other teachers or students

How Reviews Are Displayed

When you submit a review, it is tied to the school name, county, state, and principal name that you enter. Over time, this will help educators:

  • Search for a specific school and see feedback left by other staff
  • Compare overall impressions of leadership and school climate

Content and Moderation

All content on this site is user-submitted. The site itself does not verify, endorse, or fact-check individual reviews. If a review is reported or clearly violates the guidelines above, it may be edited or removed at the site owner’s discretion.

By using this site, you agree to share feedback responsibly and respectfully.

If you believe a review clearly violates these guidelines, please reach out with the school name, principal name, and a brief description so it can be reviewed.
