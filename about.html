About Rate My Principal

Rate My Principal was created as a place for teachers and school staff to share honest feedback about school leadership and school culture.

The goal is to provide transparency, encourage improvement, and help educators better understand what it is like to work at a particular school under a particular principal. This site is for educators â€” not students and not for rating classroom teachers.
