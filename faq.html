Frequently Asked Questions

How do I write a helpful review?

  • Focus on patterns over time, not one really bad (or really great) day.
  • Talk about communication, support, fairness, and overall school climate.
  • Avoid personal attacks, threats, or naming teachers or students.
  • Share what it feels like to work under this principal, not private details.

Who is this site for?

This version of Rate My Principal is designed for teachers and school staff to share their experiences working under specific principals.

Are reviews anonymous?

Reviews are anonymous only if you keep them anonymous. Please do not include your name, obvious details that clearly identify you, or anything that could identify students.

What should I include?

  • How the principal communicates with staff and families.
  • Whether staff feel supported, respected, and heard.
  • Fairness in discipline and decision-making.
  • Overall school climate and professional culture.

What should I avoid?

  • Student names or identifying details.
  • Teacher or staff names (other than the principal).
  • Threats, slurs, or harassment.
  • Accusations of crimes or illegal activity.
  • Home addresses, phone numbers, or personal contact info.

Can principals see these reviews?

Anyone who visits the site can see reviews. Please write as if the principal, your colleagues, and future educators might all read what you share.

How do I report a review?

On the main page, each review has a “Report Review” button. You can briefly explain why you think it violates the guidelines. In a future version with moderation tools, this information would help flag reviews.
